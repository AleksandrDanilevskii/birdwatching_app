What is birdwatching?

Birdwatching, or birding, is the observing of birds, either as a recreational activity or as a form of citizen science. A birdwatcher may observe by using their naked eye, by using a visual enhancement device like binoculars or a telescope, by listening for bird sounds.

Application functionality

  • Keep a record of your observations
  • Share information with other like-minded people
  • Learn about the latest interesting observations
